Biography

Homa Massih started taking piano lessons at the Tehran Music Conservatory with Gohar Abiance at the age of 8. She continued her studies with the renowned Iranian-Armenian pianist and composer Emanuel Melik Aslanian. She also extensively studied performance and piano technique from the eminent Piano Professor Dieter Weber at the Vienna Academy of Music. She received her Bachelors of Arts in piano performance and musicology from the Faculty of Fine Arts at Tehran University.

After graduation, she developed her career in teaching and performance, first in Iran, then in Germany. Presently she has been teaching piano in San Diego since 1993 and has been running her own private piano studio. She is a member of the Music Teacher's Association of California (MTAC) and has served 2 years as president and 6 years as a board member in the MTAC San Diego branch.
