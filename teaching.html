Teaching

Philosophy: Homa Massih follows the philosophy that learning piano does not only develop musical artistry but also lifelong personal and interpersonal skills. Such skills include developing self-discipline and critical thinking, as well as intellectual and spiritual harmony.

Services:

  • Teaching all levels of classical piano
    • Piano performance and technique
    • Sight reading
    • Ear training
    • Music theory
  • Individual weekly lessons
  • Quarterly group lessons
  • Yearly piano recitals
  • Preparation for the California Certificate of Merit music exam
