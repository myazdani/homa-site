Background

Homa Massih is a pianist and piano instructor currently residing in San Diego California. She has performed numerous concerts and has over 30 years teaching experience.

Teaching Philosophy: Homa Massih follows the philosophy that learning piano does not only develop musical artistry but also lifelong personal and interpersonal skills. Such skills include developing self-discipline and critical thinking, as well as intellectual and spiritual harmony.
